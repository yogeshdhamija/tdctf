- post nuclear apocalypse earth
- also post the immediate survival stage where population went down to the thousands
- now we're back up and close to being prosperous, but not quite there.

There are many different factions of humans warring over ideology but also to find specific materials from the "lost age" that we don't have the infrastructure or know how to make anymore. (These are the flags).

There will be a lot of unreliable narration, so each faction has a good side (their own propaganda) and a bad side (other factions propaganda). It will never be made 100% clear which is true.

In game, everyone starts out faction-less. You pick a faction mid game as part of your creep upgrade tree. (So you can bluff factions).

For the factions, I'm thinking:

1. "Organic, free range" humans.
   Good side: they're trying to recreate the American dream
   Bad side: eugenics, nationalism

2. Budding hive mind telepathic humans.
   Good side: it's a hive mind
   Bad side: it's a cult
   Gameplay: lots of "synergies" (towers that improve other towers, creeps that improve other creeps)

3. Technology/AI enhanced humans
   Good side: AI optimizes society
   Bad side: AI optimizes society
   Gameplay: few high powered towers/creeps but with drawbacks (location restrictions, high cost, etc)

The aesthetic will be exaggerated, so for the "organic" humans it's gonna be like 7 ft tall Greek God looking things (gold/marble towers), etc. For the hive mind stuff I was thinking fungus-y/organic thing is being genetically engineered. So like people with mushrooms on their head or something. The tech bros are gonna be cyborgs.
