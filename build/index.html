TOWER AND CREEP REFERENCE

The current configuration for towers and creeps. Reference these configuration files to see what the towers & creeps do.

  • towers.cfg — Towers
  • creep_upgrades.cfg — Creeps

LEGEND

Flag — the objective. Capture the enemy's flag and carry it to your receptacle. Flag color matches its owner (red or blue).
Spawn point (double circle) — in your color. Your creeps appear here at the start of each action phase.
Receptacle (nested squares) — in your color. Bring the stolen enemy flag here to score.
Creeps (dots) — bright small dots are light units (retrievers, scouts); dim larger dots are heavy units (melee / siege). Color = owner. A small badge like 2R1S in a cell means multiple creeps are stacked there — counts by type-code.
Slowed creep — a light-blue ring around the dot.
Flag carrier — a creep with a small flag stuck to it. The flag color is the victim's color (whose flag was stolen).
A1Tower — letter is the tower type code (look it up in towers.cfg), digit is its current level. A trailing * with dimmed color means it's still building or upgrading.
Attack beam — a brief yellow line from a firing tower to its target.
Tower HP bar — green-over-red strip beneath a tower that's taken damage.
Build zones — dark red cells are red-only, dark blue cells are blue-only. Lighter (near-black) cells are neutral: either player may build, but if both place on the same cell, the towers mutually destruct into debris.
Debris — a mid-grey cell. Non-functional but still blocks creep pathing.
